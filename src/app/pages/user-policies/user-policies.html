1. Introduction

Welcome to My Author's Hub, a comprehensive publishing platform that empowers writers to publish, market, and distribute their books worldwide. These User Policies outline the rules, guidelines, and expectations for all users of our platform, including authors, publishers, and readers.

By accessing or using our platform, you agree to comply with these policies. We reserve the right to update these policies at any time, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any changes.

We send only transactional emails related to account activity and platform notifications.

2. Account Registration and Management

2.1 Account Creation

  • You must provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration
  • You must be at least 18 years old to create an account
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials
  • One person or entity may maintain only one account unless explicitly authorized

2.2 Account Security

  • You are responsible for all activities that occur under your account
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access or security breaches
  • We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these policies

3. Content Guidelines

3.1 Acceptable Content

All content submitted to our platform must:

  • Be original or properly licensed
  • Comply with applicable copyright laws
  • Not contain defamatory, libelous, or false information
  • Not promote illegal activities or hate speech
  • Respect intellectual property rights of others

3.2 Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Content that infringes on intellectual property rights
  • Pornographic, obscene, or sexually explicit material
  • Content promoting violence, terrorism, or illegal activities
  • Spam, phishing, or fraudulent content
  • Content that violates privacy rights or contains personal information without consent

4. Publishing Guidelines

4.1 Author Responsibilities

  • Authors must ensure they have all necessary rights to publish their work
  • All manuscripts must meet our quality standards before publication
  • Authors are responsible for accuracy of book information, including ISBN, pricing, and metadata
  • Authors must respond promptly to publisher and platform communications
  • Authors must provide accurate information for ISBN generation, including title name, page count, edition, and author details
  • Authors should maintain updated profiles with current contact and bank information for royalty payments

4.2 Publisher Responsibilities

  • Publishers must verify author credentials and manuscript quality
  • Publishers are responsible for managing author relationships and contracts
  • All publishing decisions must comply with platform guidelines
  • Publishers must maintain accurate records of titles and royalties
  • Publishers must ensure proper ISBN assignment and verification before publication
  • Publishers are responsible for setting accurate pricing, royalty percentages, and distribution channels
  • Publishers must manage publishing points and ensure sufficient balance for title creation

4.3 ISBN and Title Management

  • ISBNs can be generated automatically by the platform or provided by publishers
  • Both print and ebook ISBNs are supported and must be properly assigned
  • Title information including metadata, pricing, and distribution settings must be accurate
  • Changes to published titles require update tickets and admin approval
  • ISBN barcodes are generated automatically and available for download

4.4 Update Tickets and Modifications

  • Changes to published titles require raising update tickets through the platform
  • Update tickets are required for changes to pricing, royalties, distribution, media files, and other title details
  • All update requests are subject to review and approval by platform administrators
  • Update tickets can be tracked through the Update Tickets section of your dashboard
  • Approved updates will be reflected in the platform after processing

5. Payment and Royalties

5.1 Royalty Payments

  • Royalty rates are determined by agreements between authors and publishers
  • Payments are processed according to the schedule specified in your contract
  • All royalty calculations are based on verified sales data
  • Tax obligations are the responsibility of the recipient
  • Royalties are calculated differently for print and ebook platforms, accounting for printing costs and platform margins
  • Multiple authors can receive royalties from the same title based on agreed percentages
  • Royalty calculations consider platform margins, printing costs (for print books), and flat margins

5.2 Payment Processing

  • Payments are processed through secure bank transfers to verified accounts
  • Bank account details including account number, IFSC code, and bank name must be verified before payments can be processed
  • Processing times may vary based on payment method and location
  • Any payment disputes must be reported within 30 days of the payment date
  • Earnings are tracked in your wallet and can be viewed in real-time through the dashboard
  • Payout requests are processed after verification and approval according to your payment schedule

5.3 Royalty Calculation Methods

  • For print books: Royalties are calculated after deducting platform margins, printing costs, and flat margins from the MRP
  • For ebooks: Royalties are calculated from the net sales price after platform margins
  • Royalty percentages are set per platform and can vary across different distribution channels
  • All calculations are transparent and can be viewed in the title summary and royalty reports

6. Intellectual Property Rights

All content published through our platform remains the intellectual property of the respective authors and publishers. By publishing through our platform, you grant us a non-exclusive license to:

  • Distribute and sell your work through our platform and partner channels
  • Display book covers, descriptions, and excerpts for marketing purposes
  • Process orders and manage inventory
  • Generate ISBNs and manage metadata

You retain all copyright and other intellectual property rights to your work. You may remove your content from our platform at any time, subject to existing contractual obligations.

7. Platform Usage Rules

7.1 Acceptable Use

  • Use the platform only for lawful purposes
  • Respect other users and their content
  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date profile information
  • Report any violations or suspicious activity
  • Follow proper procedures for title creation, ISBN generation, and distribution setup
  • Use update tickets for any modifications to published content
  • Maintain sufficient publishing points balance for title creation and management

7.2 Prohibited Activities

  • Attempting to hack, breach, or compromise platform security
  • Using automated systems to access the platform without authorization
  • Manipulating sales data or royalty calculations
  • Creating fake accounts or impersonating others
  • Engaging in fraudulent transactions
  • Providing false or misleading ISBN information
  • Attempting to bypass update ticket requirements for title modifications
  • Manipulating publishing points or royalty calculations

7.3 Distribution and Sales

  • All sales must be accurately recorded through the platform's sales system
  • Sales can be recorded as regular sales, live sales, or inventory entries
  • Distribution channels must be properly configured and approved
  • Platform supports multiple distribution types including MAH, National, Global, and Audiobook distribution
  • All distribution agreements must comply with platform terms and applicable laws

8. Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account if you violate these policies or engage in activities that harm the platform or other users. Upon termination:

  • Your access to the platform will be immediately revoked
  • Outstanding payments will be processed according to our payment schedule
  • Your content may be removed from the platform
  • You remain responsible for any obligations incurred before termination

9. Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute with another user or with the platform, we encourage you to contact our support team first. We will attempt to resolve disputes through mediation. If a resolution cannot be reached, disputes will be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with applicable laws.
